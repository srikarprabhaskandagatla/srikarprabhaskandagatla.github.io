About

I'm a Machine Learning Engineer and Researcher pursuing my MS in Computer Science at UMass Amherst (GPA: 3.95/4.0). My work sits at the intersection of multimodal AI, natural language processing, and scalable ML systems.

Currently, I'm a Graduate Research Extern at Dolby Laboratories, where I engineer multimodal sequential recommendation systems that fuse audio embeddings with behavioral signals — pushing the boundaries of how AI understands music at scale.

Before that, I contributed to the BioNLP Lab at UMass, fine-tuning large language models on biomedical corpora and building RAG pipelines that cut hallucination rates by 30%. I also interned at IIT Mandi, applying statistical analysis to COVID-19 mobility data to inform public health policy.

I hold a Gold Medal (First Rank) from IcfaiTech, IFHE. I build things that work in production: deployed on AWS, containerized with Docker, monitored in Grafana.
